The SATORU LICENSE INITIATIVE is a public effort to provide an open, protective, and ethical framework for cultural, symbolic, and philosophical works shared in the commons.

About the License

The SATORU PUBLIC WISDOM LICENSE (SPWL) v1.0 is designed to:

  • Protect non-commercial cultural and philosophical works from commercial exploitation
  • Preserve attribution and original meaning of shared ideas
  • Allow free reuse, translation, and non-commercial adaptation

It is inspired by the idea that not all knowledge should be for sale. Some ideas exist to be shared freely — for teaching, reflection, and collective growth.

How to Use It

Any author, thinker, developer, or educator may use this license to share their own work under similar principles. To apply it, simply include the LICENSE.md file and clearly link to this initiative:

https://github.com/satorusource/satoru-license-initiative

Vision

This license began as a way to protect the text known as Code1 and the symbolic term “SATORU” from commercial misuse, malicious distortion, and ideological appropriation. But it is not limited to one project.

Our vision is to make SPWL a recognized standard — used by other philosophers, creators, collectives, and open projects around the world. The license will remain open, versioned, and in the future — moderated through an independent structure: open, non-commercial, and non-intrusive to creativity, yet preserving the license as a repository of meaning and a point of trust.
